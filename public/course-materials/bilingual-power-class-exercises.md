## Bonus: Emergency Communication Scripts

### When you need to say no quickly:
"I appreciate you thinking of me. I'm at capacity right now. Would [alternative] work?"

### When you need to give difficult feedback:
"Can we talk about [situation]? I want to make sure we're aligned on [expectation]."

### When someone needs recognition:
"I want you to know that [specific action] made a real difference. Thank you."

### When you need to inspire action:
"Here's what's possible: [vision]. And here's how we get there: [first step]."

## Before the Live Class

Complete at least **Exercises 1, 2, and 7** and bring your questions to the session.

During the live class, you'll have the opportunity to:
- Get feedback on your practice scenarios
- Role-play challenging conversations
- Ask specific questions about your situations
- Connect with others practicing these skills

---

## After the Live Class

Review these exercises and continue practicing. Consider scheduling a weekly 15-minute self-review:
- Which languages did I use this week?
- What worked well?
- What will I try differently?
- What questions do I still have?
